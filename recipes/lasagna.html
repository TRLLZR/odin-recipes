Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian baked pasta dish with layers of meat sauce, béchamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, oregano
  • etc...

Steps

  1. Brown the meat – Fry the ground beef with onion and garlic, then add canned tomatoes and tomato paste. Let it simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Make the béchamel – Melt butter, stir in flour, then gradually add milk. Stir until smooth and creamy.
  3. Cook the pasta – Boil the lasagna sheets until al dente, then drain.
  4. Layer everything – Alternate layers of meat sauce, béchamel, pasta sheets, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake – Bake at 180°C for about 35–40 minutes until golden and bubbling on top.
