Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A hearty Italian pasta dish with rich, slow-cooked meat sauce served over al dente spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • ground Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the meat – Fry the ground beef with onion and garlic in olive oil until browned.
  2. Make the sauce – Add tomato sauce and let it simmer for 20–25 minutes until rich and thick.
  3. Cook the pasta – Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  4. Serve – Plate the spaghetti, top with bolognese sauce and finish with grated parmesan.
