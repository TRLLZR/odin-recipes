Pepperoni Pizza

Description

A classic American pizza topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and spicy pepperoni slices.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough – Stretch the pizza dough into your desired shape and place it on a baking tray.
  2. Add the sauce – Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  3. Add the toppings – Scatter mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then layer the pepperoni slices on top.
  4. Bake – Bake at 220°C for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
